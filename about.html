About Us

Who We Are

At Shivam Stonex, we specialize in premium-quality stone products, serving clients worldwide with granite, marble, and customized stone solutions. With years of experience, our mission is to combine durability and aesthetic appeal, delivering stones that transform spaces.

Customer satisfaction is at the core of what we do, offering personalized service, timely deliveries, and industry-leading standards. Our vision is to become a global leader in the stone industry.

Manufacturing Process

Step 1: Quarrying

Extracting large stone blocks from quarries while preserving the quality and natural patterns of the material.

Step 2: Cutting

Cutting the raw blocks into slabs or tiles using precision multiwire saws and gang saws.

Step 3: Shaping

Trimming and shaping the slabs into specific dimensions and forms for client requirements.

Step 4: Polishing

Polishing slabs to enhance the natural color, texture, and patterns of the stone for a flawless finish.

Step 5: Finishing

Applying finishes to customize the texture and appearance, including polished finishes.

Step 6: Packaging and Transportation

Carefully packaging slabs and tiles to ensure safe transportation to destinations worldwide.
